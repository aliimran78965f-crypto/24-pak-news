Qambar Shahdadkot Mein Shadeed Garmi

Date: 29 June 2026

Aaj Pakistan ke soobe Sindh ke shehar Qambar Shahdadkot mein garmi ka ek aur sakht din dekhne ko mila. Shahdadkot mein darja-e-hararat 47°C tak pahunch gaya. Shahdadkot ke ilawa Sindh ke kai doosre shehron mein bhi shadeed garmi ki lehar jaari rahi, jis se shehriyon ko kaafi mushkilat ka samna karna pada.
